3-Day Itinerary to Rome

(Note: Left button mouse to draw)

Day 1: Explore Ancient Rome
Start your day with a visit to the Colosseum, one of Rome's most iconic landmarks. Then, wander through the Roman Forum, where you can immerse yourself in the history of ancient Rome. In the afternoon, visit the Pantheon and marvel at its impressive dome. End your day with a stroll through the charming streets of Trastevere and enjoy a traditional Italian dinner at one of the local trattorias.

Day 2: Vatican City and Renaissance Marvels
Begin your day with a tour of Vatican City, including St. Peter's Basilica and the Vatican Museums, home to masterpieces such as Michelangelo's Sistine Chapel ceiling. Afterward, cross the river to explore the beautiful Piazza Navona and admire Bernini's Fountain of the Four Rivers. In the evening, head to the Spanish Steps area for shopping and dining.

Day 3: Baroque Rome and Hidden Gems
Spend your final day in Rome exploring the city's Baroque treasures. Visit the magnificent Trevi Fountain and toss a coin to ensure your return to Rome. Then, wander through the picturesque streets of the historic center, stopping to admire landmarks such as the Pantheon and Piazza Venezia. Don't miss the chance to visit lesser-known attractions like the Capuchin Crypt or the Aventine Keyhole for unique perspectives of the city.
