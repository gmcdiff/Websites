3-Day Itinerary to Switzerland

(Note: Left button mouse to draw)

Day 1: Alpine Adventure in Interlaken
Start your journey in Interlaken, a gateway to the stunning Swiss Alps. Spend the day exploring the picturesque town nestled between Lake Thun and Lake Brienz. Take a scenic train ride to Jungfraujoch, known as the "Top of Europe," and marvel at breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks and glaciers. Don't miss the opportunity to try outdoor activities like hiking, paragliding, or skiing.

Day 2: Lucerne and Mount Pilatus
Head to Lucerne, a charming city surrounded by mountains and situated on the shores of Lake Lucerne. Explore the well-preserved Old Town, featuring landmarks like the Chapel Bridge and Water Tower. Take a boat cruise on Lake Lucerne to admire the stunning scenery. In the afternoon, embark on an unforgettable journey to Mount Pilatus via the world's steepest cogwheel railway or cable car. Enjoy panoramic views from the summit and indulge in Swiss cuisine at one of the mountain restaurants.

Day 3: Scenic Train Ride to Zermatt
Conclude your Swiss adventure with a scenic train ride to Zermatt, home to the iconic Matterhorn mountain. Relax onboard the Glacier Express, known as the "world's slowest express train," and soak in breathtaking views of alpine landscapes, charming villages, and towering peaks. Upon arrival in Zermatt, explore the car-free village, stroll along the Bahnhofstrasse, and savor traditional Swiss dishes with a backdrop of the majestic Matterhorn.
